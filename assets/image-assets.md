# Image Assets — One More Bar & Grill

Inventory of images in this `assets/` folder, with alt-text (for accessibility + SEO), short marketing captions, and suggested usage for the rebuild. All food photos are One More's own (from their Facebook), so they're cleared to use.

> Tip for Claude Code: filenames currently contain spaces — use the **web filename** column when wiring them into the build. Several shots sit on black backgrounds and will blend seamlessly into dark sections.

---

## Food photography

### Buffalo Wings.jpg → `wings-buffalo-plate.jpg`
- **Resolution:** 1536 × 2048 (portrait) — high, hero-grade.
- **Shows:** a plate piled high with glossy whole buffalo wings in red-orange sauce, kale garnish, sauce drips on a white plate.
- **Alt text:** "A heaping plate of glossy whole buffalo wings tossed in red-orange sauce, garnished with kale."
- **Caption:** "Whole wings — not just pieces."
- **Use:** strong candidate for the **hero / pinned parallax** and the signature-items block. The portrait orientation suits a full-height hero panel.

### Buffalo Wings 2.jpg → `wings-buffalo-closeup.jpg`
- **Resolution:** 1536 × 2048 (portrait) — high.
- **Shows:** tight close-up of saucy whole wings, kale and carrot garnish, glossy texture.
- **Alt text:** "Close-up of saucy buffalo wings with a glossy sheen and kale garnish."
- **Caption:** "Sauced to order."
- **Use:** texture/detail shot — great for a tight crop, a scattered polaroid, or a background behind the mix-blend headline.

### Tails.jpg → `tails-signature.jpg`
- **Resolution:** 720 × 960 (portrait) — **lowest res; don't use full-bleed.** Best at card/thumbnail size.
- **Shows:** three boneless "Tails" (top-cut chicken breast) glossy with wing sauce on a white oval plate, lettuce garnish, black background.
- **Alt text:** "Three boneless chicken 'Tails' coated in glossy wing sauce on a white plate."
- **Caption:** "The Tails — boneless, and the reason regulars keep coming back."
- **Use:** **the signature item** — feature it on the Menu (Wings/Tails section) and call it out in copy. Black background blends into dark sections. Keep it small-to-medium given the resolution.

### Gator Bites.jpg → `gator-bites.jpg`
- **Resolution:** 1440 × 1439 (square) — good.
- **Shows:** golden fried bites with a white dipping sauce in a black ramekin and kale garnish on a white plate, dark green background.
- **Alt text:** "Golden fried Gator Bites with a creamy dipping sauce and kale garnish."
- **Caption:** "Gator Bites — order one more."
- **Use:** appetizer/shareables card; works well in a scattered-polaroid group. Square crop is flexible.

### Bruschetta Sandwich.jpg → `bruschetta-chicken-sandwich.jpg`
- **Resolution:** 2048 × 1593 (landscape) — highest res; **cut-out on a white background.**
- **Shows:** a toasted chicken bruschetta sandwich topped with diced tomato bruschetta, mozzarella and balsamic drizzle, with thick steak fries.
- **Alt text:** "Toasted chicken bruschetta sandwich topped with diced tomato, mozzarella and balsamic, served with steak fries."
- **Caption:** "Stacked, toasted, and piled with fries."
- **Use:** because it's already a clean white cut-out, it's ideal for **menu/specials cards** or a sticky card-stack panel where you want the food to float without a photo background.

### Dessert 1.jpg → `brownie-sundae-dessert.jpg`
- **Resolution:** 992 × 1333 (portrait) — good.
- **Shows:** a brownie topped with vanilla ice cream, whipped cream, chocolate-syrup drizzle and chocolate sprinkles on a white plate, black background.
- **Alt text:** "A warm brownie topped with ice cream, whipped cream and chocolate drizzle."
- **Caption:** "Save room. Or don't — get it anyway."
- **Use:** Desserts section; the black background blends into dark sections and makes a great moody scattered-polaroid or section accent.

- **Still needed:** **interior** photos (bar, wood paneling, pool tables, jukebox), **event** photos (live music, karaoke/music-bingo nights), **patio / dog-friendly** seating, and a **beer/cocktail** shot. Drop any of these in and I'll add them here.

- `Website Inspiration.html` (+ `_files`) is the saved Le Pain Quotidien page for design reference.
